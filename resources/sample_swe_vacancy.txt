## Job Description: Senior Software Engineer - Cloud Platform Team

**Company:** TechFlow Solutions  
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Remote OK)  
**Experience Level:** 5-8 years

### Job Summary
We are seeking a Senior Software Engineer to join our Cloud Platform team. You will be responsible for designing,
developing, and maintaining scalable cloud-based applications and infrastructure.

### Key Requirements
1. **Python/Java Development** - 5+ years of experience in Python or Java
2. **Cloud Platforms** - Extensive experience with AWS, Azure, or GCP
3. **Kubernetes/Docker** - Production experience with container orchestration
4. **Infrastructure as Code** - Proficiency with Terraform, CloudFormation, or similar
5. **Microservices Architecture** - Experience designing and implementing microservices
6. **Database Management** - SQL and NoSQL database experience (PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
7. **CI/CD Pipelines** - Experience with Jenkins, GitLab CI, or GitHub Actions
8. **Monitoring & Observability** - Experience with Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack
9. **Agile/Scrum Methodology** - Experience working in Agile teams
10. **Security Best Practices** - Knowledge of application and cloud security

### Nice to Have
- Experience with GraphQL APIs
- Knowledge of machine learning workflows
- Open source contributions
